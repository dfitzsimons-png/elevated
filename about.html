About Elevated

Built from the inside out.

This isn't a hospitality agency that has studied events from a distance. Elevated was built on the back of years operating VIP and guest areas at some of Ireland's largest live events.

Elevated is a specialist VIP and guest experience consultancy for live events in Ireland. The work is operational — designing and managing VIP areas, sponsor hospitality, and guest zones at large-scale concerts, festivals, and sporting events.

The founder has managed VIP operations for Live Nation and VIP Nation across multi-night concert series at Malahide Castle and Marlay Park, and has delivered guest experience programmes for events including the Irish Life Dublin Marathon. That's 10,000 to 80,000-plus attendee environments, across seasons, across formats.

The work has always been about one thing: making the premium experience actually live up to what it's supposed to be. Not on paper — on the ground, on the day, when the gates open and the plan meets reality.

Elevated works with promoters, event organisers, venues, and brands who need a specialist to take ownership of the VIP function — either as a standalone engagement or embedded within an existing production team.

No large agency behind it. No account team billing hours. You work directly with the person who'll be on site.
